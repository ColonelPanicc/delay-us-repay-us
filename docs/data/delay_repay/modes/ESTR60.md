# Delay Repay Mode :: ESTR60

ESTR 60 scheme (custom name) applies to Eurostar services only.

It applies for delays of more than 60 minutes.

Amounts can be claimed in two types: eVoucher, or cash.

## eVoucher

- 60 to 119 mins: 30% off as an evoucher
- 120 to 179 mins: 60% off as an evoucher
- 180+ mins: 75% off as an evoucher

## Cash

- 60 to 119 mins: 25% off as an evoucher
- 120 to 179 mins: 50% off as an evoucher
- 180+ mins: 50% off as an evoucher

## Discount Chart

- 30 minutes or more: 100% off the single fare.

## Time Limit

Must be claimed within three months of the delayed journey.

## Requirements

- Original Ticket required to verify.

## Where to claim

- Through website of train operator travelled with.
